Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer, who plays as a forward for Spanish club F.C. Barcelona and the Argentina national football team. Alongside Potuguese footballer, Cristiano Ronaldo, Messi is considered to be the best player in the world, both having won the Balon D'Or, a record five times each. He is the all-time leading goalscorer for both club and country, as well as the all time top scorer in La Liga.
